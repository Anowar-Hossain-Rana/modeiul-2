My Sajak Tour During winter

Author: Mr, Mizan

After reaching Khagrachhari in the morning, we will refresh ourselves and have breakfast. After that, all the vehicles will go to Baghaichhari with the permission of the army and leave for Sajek in Army Scout. After spending some time in Resort, we will go to the helipad, Konglak Hills, and Ruilui Para, here too we will spend some time. And at night there will be a Bar BQ party. Then spend the night in Sajek.On this day, we will wake up in the morning and go to the helipad to see the sunrise. We will enjoy the best morning of our life. Then we will take pictures and go to the designated Resort for breakfast by 9 o clock. After breakfast, we will leave for Khagrachhari at 10 o clock Army Scout. Lunch will be served at the famous System Restaurant. Then we will go to see Risang Jharna, Alutilaguha and Horticulture Park (Hanging Bridge). After dinner, we will take a night bus to Dhaka.
